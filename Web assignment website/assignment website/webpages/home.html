WHO ARE WE

The Johannesburg society for the prevention of cruelty to animals(JSPCA) is an animal welfare organization who was built in 1902 an is still one of the largest animal welfare organizations in South Africa. Our organization's soul purpose is the welfare of all animals not just in South Africa but the world.

MISSION

The value of animals is far higher then economic measure, we believe that animals are entitled to as much legal, moral, and ethical protection as we are and we are committed to premoting the well being of all animals in each region, while building a community that respects all living creatures.
