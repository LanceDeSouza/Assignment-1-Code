What exactly is volunteering?

The act of volunteering at JSPCA means to freely donate your time and effort to help our animal welfare organization prevent animal cruelty and promote kindness, without recieving financial compensation in return.

This may come in many forms such as...
  • Helping to clean
  • Taking walks or playing with the animals
  • Helping with administrative duties
  • Planning and helping to organise fundraises
  • Fostering animals in your own homes
